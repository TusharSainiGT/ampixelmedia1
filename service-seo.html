FAQ

Organic Growth FAQ

Quick answers to standard queries regarding search engine rankings.

How long does it take to rank on Google's first page?

For mid-to-high competition keywords, it typically takes 3 to 6 months of active SEO (fixing technical elements, writing content, earning backlinks) to show solid Page 1 rank placements. Lower difficulty long-tail phrases can rank within 30-60 days.

Will you write the articles or do we need to provide them?

We provide full-service copywriting. Our writers write SEO-optimized, highly engaging, and grammatically perfect articles based on topics approved by you. We format everything correctly with headers, tags, and internal link trees.

Do you use AI content?

We use AI to help map outlines and execute initial research, but we write and edit all final content manually. This ensures all pages rank high on Google's E-E-A-T indexes (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) and avoid system penalty downgrades.
