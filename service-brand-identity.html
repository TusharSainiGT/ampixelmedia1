FAQ

Brand Identity FAQ

Quick answers to typical queries about visual assets and vector source packages.

Do we get full copyrights and source files?

Yes. Upon final payment, 100% intellectual copyrights and vector master files (Adobe Illustrator .AI format, EPS, SVG, print-ready PDF, high-res PNGs) are fully transferred to you.

What is a brand identity book and why is it important?

A brand book is a style manual that outlines exactly how your design assets should look. It prevents partners, developers, and internal designers from stretching logos, using incorrect colors, or using mismatching typefaces in marketing materials.

Can you update an existing logo?

Yes. We offer brand refreshing services where we modernize existing logo marks, update typography choices, and refine older color schemes while retaining the legacy recognition your brand has built.
