FAQ

Development FAQ

Quick answers to typical queries about our coding and e-commerce services.

Do you build custom themes or use pre-made templates?

We write 100% custom code for premium brand pages. We do not use bloated pre-made templates that load slowly and look generic. For Shopify, we build custom sections in Liquid so your team can edit content easily while maintaining peak speed scores.

Will my website be mobile friendly and SEO optimized?

Absolutely. Responsive design is standard. We test all visual components on iOS, Android, tablets, and desktop resolutions. We also configure correct metadata tags, JSON-LD schemas, heading tags, and alt tags for indexing.

Do you offer post-launch support and security updates?

Yes. We provide 30 days of free support to resolve bugs, configure hosting anomalies, and ensure a smooth handover. We also offer monthly retainer contracts for continuous upkeep, security audits, and layout modifications.
