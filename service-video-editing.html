FAQ

Video Editing FAQ

Quick answers to typical queries about timelines, exports, and source reels.

What is your typical turnaround time?

For short-form content (Reels/Shorts), we deliver drafts in 48-72 hours. Long-form videos or direct response ads take 3-5 business days depending on complexity and graphic elements.

Can you add custom captions in multiple languages?

Yes. We offer kinetic captions in Hindi, English, Spanish, and other custom languages. We customize text styling to match your brand book guidelines.

How do revisions work?

Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions to adjust text pacing, sound volumes, color grades, or clip selections. We utilize Frame.io so you can comment on specific video frames directly.
