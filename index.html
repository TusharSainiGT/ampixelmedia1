FAQ

Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before we start growing your brand together.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with startups, personal brands, D2C brands, SaaS companies, local businesses, healthcare providers, real estate firms, educational institutions, and more. If you have ambitious growth goals, we're a great fit.

How long before I see results?

Performance marketing campaigns typically show meaningful results within 30-60 days. SEO takes 3-6 months to compound. Website development projects are delivered in 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. We're always upfront about realistic timelines.

What is your minimum budget for ad campaigns?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹30,000-₹50,000/month for meaningful results on Meta or Google. Our management fees are separate. We tailor strategies to make every budget work as efficiently as possible.

Do you offer long-term retainer packages?

Yes! Most of our clients work with us on ongoing monthly retainers for services like social media management, performance marketing, SEO, and content creation. Retainers give you priority access, dedicated resources, and better pricing than project-by-project work.

What makes Ampixel different from other agencies?

Three things: (1) We're obsessed with measurable ROI, not vanity metrics. (2) We combine AI-powered tools with human creativity for an unfair competitive advantage. (3) We treat your business like our own — your growth is our growth. We're not just an agency; we're your growth partner.

How do I get started?

Simply click "Book Free Strategy Call" and we'll schedule a 30-minute discovery call to understand your goals and see if we're the right fit. No commitment, no pressure — just a real conversation about your growth potential.

Do you provide reporting and analytics?

Absolutely. Every client gets access to a live dashboard plus weekly and monthly performance reports. We believe in complete transparency — you'll always know exactly what's happening with your campaigns, what's working, and what we're optimizing.
