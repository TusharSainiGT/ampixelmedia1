FAQ

Marketing Strategy FAQ

Quick answers to standard questions about our paid marketing services.

What platforms do you recommend for D2C/E-commerce?

For most D2C brands, we recommend starting with a strong foundation on Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram) due to its superior visual-first algorithmic target matching. This is supported by Google Search and Google Shopping (PMax) to capture immediate, high-intent searches.

Do you create the ad creatives (images, copy, videos)?

Yes. Our in-house creative division designs video edits, graphic templates, ad copy variations, and conversion assets. We align these directly with performance analytics, ensuring creatives are engineered specifically to stop the scroll.

How do you handle ad tracking after iOS 14.5 changes?

We deploy advanced Conversion API (CAPI) setups on Meta and server-side tagging on Google via Google Tag Manager. By sending secure server-to-server data, we recover lost attribution, optimize delivery algorithms, and ensure accurate campaign attribution.
