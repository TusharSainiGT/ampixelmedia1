FAQ

SMM & Creator FAQ

Quick answers to typical questions about our social media services.

Do we need to shoot our own photos and videos?

It helps, but it isn't mandatory. We source and direct professional creators to shoot lifestyle videos, product unboxings, and reviews. We also design graphic frames and write captions, so you can choose your level of involvement.

How do you choose which influencers to work with?

We analyze influencer audiences for keyword alignment, geographical details, fake follower indexes, and comment sentiment metrics. We prioritize engagement rates and historical performance over simple follower counts.

Do you manage our community messages and sales comments?

Yes. We set up comment rules and response flows to reply to sales queries (e.g., "Price?", "How to buy?") instantly. This keeps the algorithms happy and routes ready customers directly to your purchase portal.
